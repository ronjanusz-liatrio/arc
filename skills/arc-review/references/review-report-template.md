Format for `docs/review-report.md`, generated by `/arc-review` after conducting backlog health and wave alignment audits.

## Field Descriptions

### Overall Health Rating

Aggregates all findings into a single status. The rating criteria are defined in `audit-dimensions.md` and can be adjusted based on project policy.

**Critical issues** that always warrant the Critical rating:
- Any broken ROADMAP wave reference (links to non-existent ideas)
- Ideas with invalid status values (not in the allowed set)
- Bottleneck detection: many shaped ideas with zero spec-ready (indicates stalled pipeline)

**Warning-level issues** that warrant Needs Attention (when there are no critical issues):
- Stale captured ideas (> 14 days old, no Shaped timestamp)
- Priority imbalance (> 50% of ideas at one level)
- Missing brief fields in shaped ideas
- Orphaned spec-ready ideas (not assigned to waves)
- Stub-only VISION or CUSTOMER (exists but minimal content)

### Backlog Health Findings

Five checks that audit the quality and maturity of the backlog:

1. **Stale ideas** — Captured ideas that have sat unshapen for longer than the configured threshold (default: 14 days)
2. **Priority imbalance** — When the distribution across P0-P3 is skewed (e.g., all ideas are P0)
3. **Status distribution** — Pipeline health snapshot; reveals bottlenecks if many ideas cluster at one stage
4. **Missing brief fields** — Shaped ideas that lack one or more of the seven required brief sections
5. **Invalid status values** — Ideas with status values not in {captured, shaped, spec-ready, shipped}

### Wave Alignment Findings

Six checks that verify consistency between BACKLOG, ROADMAP, VISION, and CUSTOMER:

1. **Broken ROADMAP refs** — Wave entries that reference BACKLOG ideas by anchor where the anchor doesn't exist
2. **Status mismatch** — Ideas referenced in ROADMAP waves that haven't reached spec-ready or shipped status
3. **Orphaned spec-ready** — Ideas with spec-ready status not assigned to any ROADMAP wave
4. **VISION alignment** — VISION.md must exist and contain substantial content (not auto-created stub)
5. **CUSTOMER alignment** — If shaped ideas reference personas, those personas must exist in CUSTOMER.md
6. **Cross-reference integrity** — BACKLOG summary table rows must match the `## {Title}` sections (and vice versa)

### Status Distribution Summary

Shows the count of ideas at each lifecycle stage. The "Pipeline Health" subsection interprets the distribution:

- **Capture → Shape flow**: Ratio of captured to shaped ideas. A high ratio suggests ideas aren't being prioritized for shaping.
- **Shape → Spec-Ready flow**: Ratio of shaped to spec-ready ideas. Reveals if ideas are getting stalled at the brief stage.
- **Spec-Ready → Shipped flow**: Ratio of spec-ready to shipped ideas. Shows how quickly ideas move through implementation.

### Recommended Actions

Grouped by severity: Critical (must address), Warnings (before next wave), Info (nice-to-have).

Each action should be:
- **Specific**: "Mark 3 stale ideas with reviewed comment" not "Clean up backlog"
- **Actionable**: Include who should do it and how
- **Linked to findings**: Reference the check that surfaced it

### Fixes Applied

A checklist of interactive fixes that the user authorized during this review session. Each fix represents a modification to BACKLOG.md or ROADMAP.md that `/arc-review` applied with user confirmation.

The checklist helps stakeholders understand which specific corrections were made without reviewing git diffs.
